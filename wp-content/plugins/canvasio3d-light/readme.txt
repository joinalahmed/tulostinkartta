3D Plugin for WordPress - Shows easily your 3D-Models with WebGL in the format: .stl .obj .dae

== Description ==

<h1>With Canvasio3D Light can you show your 3D Models in your WordPress website.</h1><br>Upload your 3D-Models easily with the WordPress Media Manager on your website, in the format: .obj & .mtl, .stl and .dae. for 3D-Printing, engineering, or simple your products as interactive 3D-Models.<br><br>Insert a Short-Code in a single side with one click on the 3D-Icon above to the WordPress Text editor. Canvasio3D Light use the 3D-Engine "three.js" for WebGL.<br><br>For Support visit the Forums at http://www.canvasio3d.com/en/forums/

== Installation ==

Installing the plugin takes less than one minute. 

1. Download "canvasio3d-light.zip" and install the plugin

2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress

3. Use the "3D"-Icon in your WordPress Text editor to insert a Short-Code for your 3D-Model
 

Compatibility: The plugin is compatible with WordPress 3.9.0 or higher.
